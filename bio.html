Biography

Theodore Tan

Born in the small island-nation of Singapore, Theodore spent his younger days trying to come to terms with the constant humidity of a country lying one-degree north of the equator, as well as attempting to pick up skills that he would need later on in life. Growing up, he always imagined that he would become an author destined to churn out the next New York Times Bestseller or Man Booker Prize Winner. To make that dream a reality, he would staple haphazardly cut sheets of drawing block paper together and pretend that he was publishing his own (handwritten) novel.

Unfortunately, as life would have it, Theodore discovered that writing was hard and attaining any form of recognition through writing was even harder. As such, he meandered through Middle and High School trying to figure out what interested him enough to pursue for the rest of his life. Eventually, after dabbling in advanced math and theatre studies among other things, he finally settled on studying the discipline of History at the Undergraduate level.

Now, Theodore is a History and Computer Science double-major after his encounters with a newfound love for coding and problem solving. In his (very limited) spare time, he can be found in the gym engaging in some form of sports, out and about capturing moments with his Canon DSLR, or finding a quiet spot to revert back to his first love -- writing.
